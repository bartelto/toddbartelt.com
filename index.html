About Me

I’m a software developer with a mathematics and engineering background, currently adding full-stack web development to my list of skills through a program at KU Professional and Continuing Education. My strengths include troubleshooting, clear and precise communication, and teamwork.

What energizes me? I love team-based, creative problem-solving and creating software applications that not only work, but have a good look and feel, too. Back in my factory automation days, I developed dozens of successful machine vision software solutions using VB.NET and C#, creating valuable intellectual property that helped win new customers in the process.

I am patient, insightful, and dedicated to exceeding expectations while still making sure the whole team crosses the finish line together. I’m also open to new opportunities, so please contact me!
